About Papua New Guinea

   Gaining its independence in 1975, Papua New Guinea is a relatively new, and small 
nation in southeast Asia that shares an island with its bordering country, Indonesia.
The country itself is not exceptionally big, with an area of 178,704 square miles, which
is roughly the size of the states of Montana and Maine combined. Its capital city is Port
Moreby but Papua New Guinea also has other major cities like Lae,Goroka, Wewak, and Madang.
Aside from its geography, the unique people of this nation speak multiple languages. Some of 
these languages include Hiri Motu, Tok Pisin, and even English.
